DESIGN WORK

Selected design projects from MMA 100 (Visual Foundations) at BMCC. Logo design, figure ground work, and photo compositing.

These projects come from MMA 100 (Visual Foundations) at BMCC. Each one focused on a different design fundamental.

The Peak Bikes logo was built in Illustrator using geometric shapes to form a bike silhouette inside a mountain peak, shown in black and white and in color. The Figure Ground Rhythm piece uses overlapping elephant and crane silhouettes to explore how color, layering, and negative space interact. The Comic Arts banner is a Photoshop compositing exercise that combines multiple illustration styles into a single event poster.
