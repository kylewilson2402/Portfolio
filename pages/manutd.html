Fan Site · Multi-Page

MANCHESTER
UNITED HUB

A fan website covering the history, legends, trophies, and spirit of the most decorated club in English football.

View Live Site →
This site was built as a love letter to Manchester United Football Club. It covers the full scope of the club's history from foundation in 1878 through the Busby era, the Munich air disaster, Sir Alex Ferguson's dynasty, and beyond.

The design is bold and unapologetic. Dark backgrounds, United red accents, strong typography. Each page covers a different part of the club. The home page sets the tone with Old Trafford imagery and stats, the Legends page profiles icons like Best, Charlton, Cantona, and Keane, and the Trophies section lays out the full honours list.

Built as a responsive multipage site with real photography and a distinct section for every part of the club.
