About Me

I'm Kyle Wilson, a multimedia designer and developer studying Multimedia Programming & Design at BMCC. I'm the founder of Kyonic Designs — a freelance creative brand built on bold design and clean execution.

My work lives at the intersection of web development and production design. I don't just build websites — I build the entire brand ecosystem around them. Whether that's a responsive fan site, a set of social media templates, a pitch deck, or a full brand guideline document, I deliver work that's consistent, professional, and ready to use.

Outside of design, you'll find me deep into boxing, watching Manchester United, writing stories, or sketching out the next big idea.
