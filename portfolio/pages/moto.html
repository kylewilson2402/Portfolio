MIND OF
THEIR OWN

A promotional website for Brandasund Peterson's independent animated short film — robots born from the ashes of a crumbled city, searching for humanity's last survivors.

View Live Site →
Mind of Their Own is an independent animated short about a colony of robots that rises from the ashes of a crumbled city in response to a far away radio signal. A team of five robots are sent on a mission to find and save the last remaining humans.

The site was designed to match the film's atmosphere. Dark, cinematic, layered. The typography sits on top of the character art and the whole thing is meant to feel like you're actually inside the world of the film.

My role was Web Developer & Production Assistant. I took the film's visual identity and turned it into a full website and promotional materials.
